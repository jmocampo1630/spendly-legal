What cloud deletion covers

Permanent deletion removes your account-linked cloud data, including:

  • your Supabase authentication account and profile;
  • account-owned credits and paid entitlements;
  • scan requests and temporary parsed scan results; and
  • account-linked scan usage rows.

What this page cannot delete

Remote deletion cannot reach an old phone, tablet, or computer. Clear Spendly's app storage or uninstall Spendly on every device where local budgets, reminders, settings, caches, or notifications should be removed.

Backup files you exported to Downloads, Drive, email, or another location are separate copies and remain until you delete them yourself.

A pseudonymous device free-scan record may remain for up to 90 days for fraud prevention. It is not an account and cannot restore deleted account or budgeting data.
